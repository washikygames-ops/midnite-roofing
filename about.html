Who We Are

About Us

Our Story

Wellington's
Trusted Roofers

Midnite Roofing are residential and commercial roofing specialists based in Petone, Lower Hutt. We cover the full Greater Wellington Region — from Lower Hutt and Upper Hutt through to Wellington City and Tawa.

We specialise in new roof installations, full roof replacements and recladding, and roof repairs. Whatever your roofing needs, our team handles it from start to finish.

We're known for going above and beyond — working late, finding the root cause of problems, and not leaving until the job is done right.
